colour information.

for the Epson LX-80 printer (
alterations te make the routine work
on the EP 80+ and NT 80+ printers. are
on kines 4 and 5). The routine will
work on any printer that can set its

graphics by first sending an escape
sequence followed by a 16 bit number

graphics to follow, followed by the
graphics bytes where bit 7 corresponds
to the top pin on the print head. On
the LX-80, I have used the escape
sequence ESC,"#",5 which. is the 1:1
graphics aode (572 points per line).

after printing, the sequence ESC,*2"
is used» [ think these sequences
should work on most Epson compatible
